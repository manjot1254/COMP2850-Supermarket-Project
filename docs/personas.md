# Personas

## Persona 1 — Customer

**Name:** Sarah Johnson  
**Role:** Online Customer  

### Description
Sarah regularly shops online and wants a simple and efficient way to browse and purchase products.

### Goals
- Browse available products
- Add products to basket
- Complete checkout easily
- View previous orders

### Frustrations
- Complicated checkout
- Difficult navigation

### Needs
- Product browsing interface
- Basket system
- Order history
- Account management
